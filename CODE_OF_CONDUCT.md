# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment.

Examples of expected behavior:
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical content.
- Accept feedback and engage professionally.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or trolling.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.
They may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate these rules.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact:
- `dfr@esmad.ipp.pt`

All reports will be reviewed and handled promptly and fairly.
